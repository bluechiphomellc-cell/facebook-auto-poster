How to set up Facebook Auto Poster

The whole setup takes about 20–30 minutes. No coding. No developer accounts. Just follow the steps below.

1

Download and Install

After purchasing, download FAP from your account dashboard. Open the program and follow the on-screen setup prompts. That's the hard part. It wasn't that hard.

2

Connect Your Facebook Page

Click "Connect Facebook" inside the app. Log in with the Facebook account that manages your page. Select which page(s) you want FAP to post to. Grant the necessary permissions when prompted.

FAP only needs permission to post on your behalf. We don't read your messages, access your personal profile, or do anything weird.

3

Use the Reddit Image Scraper

Go to the Scraper tab inside the app.

  1. Paste the URL of any subreddit (example: reddit.com/r/pugs)
  2. Choose how many images you want to pull (up to 1,000)
  3. Pick your filter: Top All Time, Top Monthly, Top Weekly, Hot, or New
  4. Click Scrape

The images will download to a folder inside the app.

Important: Review the images before scheduling. Remove anything you don't want posted.
4

Schedule Your Posts

Go to the Scheduler tab.

  1. Select the folder of images you want to post
  2. Choose your posting times (example: 8:30am and 11:45am daily)
  3. Set your start date and how many days you want to schedule
  4. Click Schedule

Done. FAP will post automatically at the times you selected. You can check the dashboard anytime to see what's been posted and what's coming up.

5

Ongoing Use

When your scheduled posts run out, just scrape more content and schedule another batch. The whole process takes about 15–20 minutes every few weeks.

If any posts fail, the dashboard will show you which ones. You can retry them with one click.

!

Troubleshooting

Posts aren't going through

Make sure your Facebook connection is still active. Facebook occasionally requires you to re-authorize apps. Reconnect inside FAP and reschedule.

Images aren't scraping

Double check that the subreddit URL is correct and that the subreddit actually has image posts. Some subreddits are mostly text.

Something else is broken

Email us at support@facebook-auto-poster.com. We'll help you fix it. That's what the 7-day guarantee is for.
