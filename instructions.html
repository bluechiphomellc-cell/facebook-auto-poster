Instructions

Getting started with FAP

From download to your first scheduled post. The whole process takes about 10 minutes.

1

Download & Install

After purchasing, you'll get a download link for your operating system (Mac or Windows).

  • Windows: Run the installer (FacebookAutoPoster_Setup.exe). Click Next a few times. A shortcut will appear on your Desktop.
  • Mac: Unzip the download. You'll get Facebook Auto Poster.app. Drag it to your Applications folder or just double-click it.
Note for Mac users: The first time you open the app, macOS may say it's from an "unidentified developer." Right-click the app, select "Open," then click "Open" again on the dialog. You only need to do this once.
2

Activate your license

When you first open FAP, it'll ask for your license key. This is the key you received in your purchase confirmation email from our store.

  1. Copy your license key from your email
  2. Paste it into the activation field
  3. Click Activate

That's it. The app will verify your subscription and unlock.

3

Get your content ready

You need a folder of images on your computer. This is the content FAP will post to your page. You can use any images you want, but the fastest way to build a library is the Reddit image scraper (included with your purchase).

  1. Open the Reddit scraper
  2. Paste in a subreddit URL (e.g. https://reddit.com/r/NatureIsFuckingLit)
  3. Pick a filter: Top All Time, Top Monthly, Top Weekly, Hot, or New
  4. Set how many images you want
  5. Click scrape and let it run
Important: Screen your images before posting. Remove anything low quality, watermarked, or that could violate Facebook's community standards. It only takes a few minutes and saves you headaches later.
4

Connect your Facebook page

Inside FAP, click "+ Add Page" in the sidebar. This opens a Facebook login window.

  1. Log in with the Facebook account that manages your page
  2. Grant the requested permissions (FAP needs permission to post to your page)
  3. Select which page(s) you want to connect

Your connected pages will appear in the sidebar. You can add as many pages as you want.

5

Set your schedule & go

For each page, you'll configure:

  • Media folder: Point it to the folder of images you prepared in Step 3
  • Post times: Add the times you want posts to go out each day (e.g. 9:00 AM, 12:00 PM, 6:00 PM)
  • Posting days: How many days into the future to schedule
  • Captions: Choose auto-generated captions, custom captions from a file, or no captions

Once everything's set, click "Schedule Next Batch" and FAP will schedule your posts across the days and times you selected. You'll see a progress bar as it works.

When it's done, your posts are scheduled directly on Facebook. They'll go out on time even if your computer is off. You're done.

Pro tip: Facebook allows scheduling up to 30 days out. When your current batch is running low, open FAP and schedule the next batch. The whole process takes about 2 minutes once your content folder is ready.
