Getting started

1
Download and install. On Mac, open the .app file. On Windows, run the installer. If your OS warns you about an unrecognized app, click "More info" then "Run anyway" (totally normal for new software).
2
Enter your license key. You received it in your purchase confirmation email from LemonSqueezy. Copy and paste it into the activation screen.
3
Connect your Facebook pages. Click "Add Page" and log into Facebook. Select the pages you want to manage. All your pages, including Business Manager pages, will show up.
4
Start scheduling. Pick a content folder, set your post times, and hit "Schedule Next Batch." Check out the full instructions for a detailed walkthrough.
