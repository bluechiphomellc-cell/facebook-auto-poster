About

We built this because we were tired of doing it by hand.

Facebook Auto Poster started as a personal project. We'd been running Facebook pages for over a decade and got tired of the one thing that never changed: you had to show up every single day and post.

That's fine when it's your passion. It's less fine when it's your fifth page and you're staring at a content calendar at 11pm on a Tuesday.

So we automated it. We built a tool that scrapes content, schedules posts, and keeps pages active without anyone having to babysit them.

Then we realized other page owners probably had the same problem. So here we are.

FAP is a small company. We're not backed by venture capital. We don't have a boardroom. We just have a product that works and a name that makes people do a double take. Both of those are intentional.

If you need help or have questions, reach out to us at support@facebook-auto-poster.com.
